Who We Are?


PA Plus Construction Co., Ltd. Has been one of Thailand’s most trusted experienced-fabricator since its establishment in 2016. Our engineers have extensive experience of pipe welding, fabrication, modification and other services in over 100 projects. We also supply skilled manpower support to companies looking for experienced welders and fabricators. We aim to offer a comprehensive, one-stopservice solution for our clients. Our office is currently based in Chonburi, eastern of Thailand.

We are proud to have served a wide range of major projects including fabrication works for Chevron, CPOC, Danieli, GPS Bahrain, Emco, CUEL, etc across various parts of Thailand. Our scope of work also extends beyond fabrication and welding in factories. Our welding team has experience in industrial estates, oil depots, roadworks and more. They have been trained for the highest safety standards and commit to on-time project delivery. To provide piece of mind to our clients, all our work have 365-day warranty. We also have a dedicated team to cater urgent tasks or projects as required by clients.
